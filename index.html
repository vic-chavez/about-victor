From the Coast Back to the Desert

My name is Victor. I'm 46 years old and I have recently moved from San Diego back to Albuquerque, where I was born. Upon moving from CA I also decided to switch career fields. This is my 3rd career in 26 years. My first career was a Chemical Operation Specialist in the US Army for 9 years spanning from 1996 to 2005. My second career was Instructional design, instructor, and e-learning developer from 2005-2020.

I have many hobbies but most recently I developed a joy for 3D printing, primarily miniatures. I enjoy motorcycle riding and have a great passion for music. I have played the cello since I was 10 and am currently learning the guitar and tongue drum.

I am divorced and do not have children, however I do have 3 small furry kids that I love to death. As you can see in the first picture, they are quite the hams.
